# General Engine Inspection Checklist

## Pre-Inspection Requirements
- Engine must be cooled for minimum 2 hours
- All safety lockouts must be applied
- Maintenance log must be reviewed

## Standard Inspection Items
1. Visual inspection of engine nacelle
2. Fan blade inspection for FOD damage
3. Oil level and quality check
4. Fuel filter condition check
5. Engine mount inspection
6. Exhaust nozzle inspection
7. Sensor calibration verification

## Documentation Requirements
- Record all sensor baselines before and after maintenance
- Update engine health monitoring system
- File maintenance report within 24 hours
- Update remaining useful life estimate

## Safety Notes
- Never perform inspection on running engine
- Use approved PPE at all times
- Follow all lockout/tagout procedures
